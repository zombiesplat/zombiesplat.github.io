Raffle Tools

  • Non Profit Fundraising Toolbox

Problem: Your golf tournament is a fundraiser, but everyone is busy networking and golfing that the fundraising takes a back seat.

Solution: During registration, participants download the app or register a phone number for SMS alerts. Each hole's sponsor can interact with the foursome via the app. Participants receive updates of bids or wins immediately. Everyone can stay engaged with the fundraising while never leaving the course.
